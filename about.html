About Us

Welcome To Madeena Beach Hotel

Our Story

Located at 502 Matara Road, Katugoda, Galle, Madeena Beach Hotel offers a unique blend of Sri Lankan and Chinese cuisine, including specialties like Kottu, String Hoppers, and traditional curries. We pride ourselves on delivering unforgettable flavors and warm hospitality to all our guests.

Open daily with flexible dining hours, our restaurant is the perfect place to enjoy authentic meals in a relaxed atmosphere. Whether you're here for breakfast, lunch, or dinner, our menu is crafted to satisfy every palate.
